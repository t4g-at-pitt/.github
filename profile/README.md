Welcome to the official GitHub organization for **Tech for Good (T4G)** at the University of Pittsburgh.  
We are dedicated to **growing the responsible technology community** through learning, career growth, and research enrichment.

#### Join our Discord: https://discord.gg/7ZzZ7PmaXc

---

## What We Do
- **Hands-on development**: Work on projects that have social impact and encourage ethical design.  
- **Learning & Research**: Explore the world of research from students and instructors.
- **Career Growth**: Share resources and opportunities that help members learn and grow into responsible technologists.  
- **Events & Community**: Host workshops, discussions, and collaboration spaces for students of all majors and skill levels.  

## Contributing
We welcome contributions from all members of the Pitt community, regardless of experience level. To contribute, please fork the repository or create a separate branch before making changes. Once your work is ready, submit a pull request with a commit messages, and a T4G admin will review your code.
